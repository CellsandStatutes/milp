NSW Mental Health Timeline (1850–2025)

Key interventions, reforms, and reports in mental health policy

1866

Gladesville Hospital becomes a central psychiatric institution in NSW.

1958

NSW Mental Health Act passed, modernising psychiatric care and legal oversight.

1983

Richmond Report recommends deinstitutionalisation and community-based care.

2007

Mental Health Act 2007 introduced, replacing the 1990 Act and strengthening patient rights.

2012

NSW Mental Health Commission established to oversee reform and policy development.

2014–2024

NSW Mental Health Reform Plan launched to improve service quality and access.

2020–2025

NSW Aboriginal Mental Health and Wellbeing Strategy implemented.

2025

NSW Suicide Monitoring System Report released; HASI and CLS programs reviewed.
